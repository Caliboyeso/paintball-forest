Rules!

  1. Set your marker to shoot at a maximum velocity of 300 fps.
  2. Never remove your mask on the field for any reason.
  3. Always respect your teammates and opponents.
  4. Don’t act like you own the field; give everyone a chance to get in on the action.
  5. Never blind fire. If you can’t see what you’re shooting at, don’t shoot.
  6. Paintballs can hurt at close range, even with protective gear!
  7. When you’re hit, you’re hit. Don’t wipe paint off and keep playing; this is considered cheating.
  8. Raise your arms and marker so other players know you’ve been hit.
  9. The referee’s ruling is the final word.
  10. We know it’s a competition and you want to win, so strategize the heck out of the game!
